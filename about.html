About Q World Athletics

Discover our story, our values, and the people behind our mission to empower youth through the transformative power of athletics.

Our Story

Q World Athletics Inc. was founded with a simple but powerful vision: every young person deserves the chance to discover their potential through sport. Based in Olney, Maryland, our organization grew from a grassroots passion for youth development into a recognized 501(c)(3) nonprofit serving hundreds of young athletes and families.

Under the leadership of founder Albert M. Quashie, we have built a community-driven organization that uses track & field as a vehicle for teaching discipline, resilience, teamwork, and leadership — skills that extend far beyond the finish line.

Mission, Vision & Values

🎯

Our Mission

To empower youth through athletics, mentorship, and community engagement — building champions in sport and in life.

👁

Our Vision

A world where every young person has access to quality athletic programs and the mentorship they need to thrive.

❤️

Our Values

Integrity, inclusivity, excellence, community, and the belief that athletics can transform lives and unite neighborhoods.

What We Believe

We believe athletics is more than competition — it is a pathway to personal growth, academic success, and community connection. Our approach is rooted in:

  • Access and equity — removing financial barriers to participation
  • Holistic development — nurturing mind, body, and character
  • Family involvement — engaging parents and guardians as partners
  • Community impact — strengthening neighborhoods through shared purpose

Our Journey

Key milestones in the Q World Athletics story.

2019

The Beginning

Albert M. Quashie establishes Q World Athletics with a small group of dedicated volunteers and a vision for youth empowerment.

2020

501(c)(3) Status Granted

Q World Athletics receives official nonprofit status, enabling tax-deductible donations and formal partnerships.

2021

First Youth Track Season

Our inaugural track & field season launches with 50 young athletes participating in training and local competitions.

2023

Mentorship Program Launch

We expand beyond athletics to offer academic mentorship, pairing student-athletes with experienced mentors.

2025

500+ Athletes Served

A landmark year — we surpass 500 youth athletes served and host our largest community athletic showcase.
