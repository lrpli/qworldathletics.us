Our Programs

From track & field training to academic mentorship, our programs are designed to develop the whole athlete — mind, body, and character.

What We Offer

Every program is built on our core belief that athletics is a powerful vehicle for positive youth development.

🏅
Ages 7 – 18

Youth Track & Field

Our flagship program provides year-round training in sprints, distance running, hurdles, jumps, and throws. Athletes of all skill levels are welcome — from beginners discovering the sport to competitive athletes preparing for meets.

  • Expert coaching from certified instructors
  • Seasonal training cycles with periodization
  • Local, regional, and national competition opportunities
  • Equipment and uniforms provided
📚
Grades 3 – 12

Academic Mentorship

We pair student-athletes with experienced mentors who provide one-on-one academic support, goal-setting guidance, and life skills development. We believe champions are made in the classroom as well as on the track.

  • Weekly tutoring and homework support
  • Goal-setting workshops
  • College readiness and scholarship guidance
  • Report card incentive program
☀️
Summer

Summer Athletic Camps

Intensive week-long camps combining track & field training with team-building activities, nutrition education, and fun. A highlight of the summer for young athletes across the region.

  • Half-day and full-day options available
  • Age-appropriate skill development
  • Guest appearances from elite athletes
  • Scholarships available for families in need
🌎
All Ages

Community Events

We bring neighborhoods together through athletic showcases, family fun runs, health fairs, and community fitness days. These events promote healthy living and strengthen local connections.

  • Free community fun runs and walks
  • Health and wellness screenings
  • Athletic showcase and awards ceremonies
  • Family-friendly activities and food
💪
Ages 13 – 18

Leadership Development

Our leadership track empowers teen athletes to become role models and community leaders. Participants gain public speaking, team management, and service-learning experience.

  • Youth leadership council
  • Public speaking and presentation skills
  • Community service project planning
  • Peer mentoring opportunities
👩‍🎓
Parents & Coaches

Coach & Parent Workshops

We invest in the adults who support our athletes. Our workshops cover youth sport psychology, nutrition, injury prevention, and how to create positive competitive environments.

  • Sport psychology for youth coaches
  • Nutrition and hydration fundamentals
  • Positive parenting in youth sports
  • First aid and injury prevention
📝

How to Join

Getting started with Q World Athletics is easy. We welcome athletes of all skill levels and backgrounds. Financial need is never a barrier — scholarships and fee waivers are available.

  • Contact us or visit our events to learn more
  • Complete a registration form for your child
  • Attend an orientation session with our coaching staff
  • Join the team and start training!
